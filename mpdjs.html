MPDjs Support

MPDjs is a MPD (Music Player Daemon) client providing remote control over all your MPD based songs

Features:

  • Random Playlist generation
  • Multiple connections
  • Drill down through artists and albums to your songs
  • Quickly search for songs
  • Find songs by Artist, Album, Song Search or File
  • Swipe left or right for page selection or tap header bar to access page menu
  • Output Selection
  • Server Discovery via Bonjour

Requirements :

An MPD Server running the latest version (preferably 0.20.x or greater) in your home network. See www.musicpd.org for more details

iOS Version 10.2 and above

Support :

For support questions send an email to richardabackhouse@gmail.com providing details of the MPD server you are connecting to and details of your MPD database (number of artists/albums/song etc)
